School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCOPE)


Introduction


Computer Science Engineering is a field of engineering that has grown to be a major obliging field. Computer science engineering has taken a good path towards establishing a strong position in the field of engineering. VIT's School of Computer Science and Engineering was established in 2010. Since then, VIT has produced a large number of computer science engineers who are capable of meeting the needs of IT firms. VIT Chennai is the best engineering college for computer science and has maintained an excellent reputation for many years.



Vision

To be a world-renowned centre of education, research and service in computing and allied domains.

Mission

    To offer computing education programs with the goal that the students become technically competent and develop lifelong learning skills.

    To undertake path-breaking research that creates new computing technologies and solutions for industry and society at large.

    To foster vibrant outreach programs for industry, research organizations, academia and society.

Opportunities

If you graduate from the best engineering college for computer science with good skills, you can work at top firms with titles such as software developer, web developer, computer system analyst, computer, data engineer, and many more. Students have a plethora of opportunities and must be willing to learn and update themselves in this creative community.

Goals

    VIT strives to achieve its goal of exhibiting excellence in terms of teaching and also to produce graduates who will be assets to society

    To produce graduates who can reach their full potential in designing and developing systems that involve integrating software and hardware devices

    To evoke a sense of determination and perseverance to achieve excellence in the students.

Specializations

    Theoretical Computer Science

    Data Analytics

    Software Engineering

    Cyber-Physical Systems

    Artificial Intelligence

International Space Science Challenge 2022,


It is pleasure to share that our team ‘Quarky Quasars’ has achieved the position of ‘Winner’ in the Space Robotics Category in the International Space Science Challenge 2022, organized by Spaceonova with an objective to induce problem-solving skills, design thinking, and solution-oriented mindset among students in the fields of space technology and exploration.


The team won against 100+ competing teams with the proposed idea of ‘Asteroid mining bots’, which suggested ways to exploit asteroids that are filled with potential minerals that can be beneficial for earthlings in order to solve the problem of lack of resources. The project includes planning and designing the technical aspects of Mining Bots using prototyping software.
